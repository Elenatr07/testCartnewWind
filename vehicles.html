The Toyota HiAce Commuter offers a comfortable interior with seating for up to 14 passengers: 1 next to the driver, 3 in each of the second, third, and fourth rows, and 4 in the fifth row. The fifth-row seats can be folded for extra luggage space. The dashboard features manual AC controls, and each seat is equipped with a seatbelt for safety.

The Toyota Premio is a luxurious commercial passenger vehicle with 14 seats, designed for comfortable city and intercity travel. It features Hill-Start Assist to prevent rollback on inclines, Vehicle Stability Control for better handling, dual SRS airbags, and 7 V Point seatbelts with pretensioners and force limiters for enhanced safety.

The Toyota Innova is a mid-range car equipped with comprehensive security and safety features for driver and passenger protection. The first row of seats includes a reclining and sliding system. The second row offers a one-touch tumble feature for easy access to the third row. The third row has space-up and reclining features to expand trunk space.
